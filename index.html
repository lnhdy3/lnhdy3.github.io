Fruit Drums

Using an Adafruit Circuit Playground, Alligator Clips, Arduino IDE, and Hydrogen Application
(for receiving MIDI signals and drum sounds), I was able to create music!
This project was fun and was my first venture into hardware. I had a lot of fun customizing the drum sounds
and selecting the fruit to be used. I used the capacitive touch capabilities of the Circuit Playground and attached the stems
of the fruit via alligator clips. By hooking up the allligator clips to the Circuit Playground I was able to transmit MIDI
signals over USB, which was then processed by the Hydrogen Application to produce my drum sounds.

PickHacks Hackathon - Unity Game (Bunny Gains)

Bunny Gains is a Unity game I created during a hackathon. The theme of the hackathon was wellness, me and my team
chose the financial wellness track and created Bunny Gains to teach users financial literacy through a stock market simulator.
The player picks up berries in exchange for cash. Bread acts as a stock and is varried in price so the player would sell bread for cash.
This project was a great learning experience and was my teams first time using Unity and C#, I learned a lot
about team work and working under a time constraint through this project and
look forward to participating in more hackathons in the future!
